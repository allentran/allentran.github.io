June 14, 2015

Like pretty much everyone, I am obsessed with word embeddings word2vec or GloVe.

Key to algorithms is that 1) most sources of words are sequential (the primary source of words is sentences) and 2) the words surrounding a given word are meaningful. The ability to derive meaning from the structure of words is why word embedding models train to predict a word given the context surrounding it (or vice versa). Moreover, this training is super successful because there is so much free labelled data.
